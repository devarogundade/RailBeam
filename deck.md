# Beam — pitch deck (outline)

Short-form copy you can lift slide-by-slide. Product name as shipped in the app: **Beam**.

---

## Title

**Beam**  
*The workspace where money moves with AI agents — on 0G.*

---

## The problem

Finance and operations teams already use AI for drafts and research, but **money still lives in a different universe**: wallets, treasuries, invoices, compliance checks, and agent tools rarely share one thread of truth. Hiring “an agent” off a generic chat product does not give you **on-chain hires, subscriptions, and receipts** tied to the same identity you use at work.

**Crypto-native operators** face the same fragmentation: balances, agent spend, and settlement status are scattered across explorers, dashboards, and chat UIs. There is no single place that combines **conversation, specialist agents, and traceable settlement** without bolting together half a dozen products.

---

## What we solve

Beam is **agentic finance in one workspace**: you fund work, hire specialists from a catalog, collaborate in chat, and keep **balances, hires, and receipts aligned** — including chain-anchored subscriptions and payment flows — so you always know what happened and what it cost.

---

## The product (three pillars)

1. **Conversation**  
   Natural-language collaboration with the agents you have hired. Switch agents without throwing away context when the job changes.

2. **Marketplace**  
   Discover and hire agents by domain — e.g. payments, taxes, reporting, DeFi, compliance — with **monthly hire on 0G** and clear Agent IDs that stay consistent across catalog, chat, and chain.

3. **Treasury / dashboard**  
   One view for **wallets, agent activity, subscriptions, and settlement** — including paths for card funding, on-ramps, and KYC where the product needs regulated rails — so operational money is not invisible next to “the AI.”

---

## How it works (buyer story)

- Connect and complete onboarding once.  
- **Browse → hire → chat**: the same Agent ID follows the relationship.  
- Pay or settle through the flows the product exposes (wallet, payment links, card/on-ramp where enabled).  
- Review activity and artifacts in one dashboard instead of reconciling chat logs against a separate treasury.

---

## Why 0G (trust layer)

Beam is positioned for **high-trust agentic finance**:

- **TEE-backed compute** when sensitive reasoning should stay inside an enclave.  
- **0G chain** to anchor hires and money movement.  
- **0G storage** for durable exports, files, and long-lived context so the workspace stays coherent over time.

That stack is the answer to “Can I run serious financial workflows on an agent platform?” — not only UX, but **where data lives and how commitments are recorded**.

---

## Differentiation (plain language)

| Typical stack | Beam |
|---------------|------|
| General chat + separate wallet app | Chat + **hired agents** + **treasury** together |
| No standard agent identity across surfaces | **Agent ID** consistent across catalog, thread, and chain |
| Hard to audit what an agent did vs what was paid | Flows designed to keep **work and settlement** inspectable |
| One-size AI | **Specialist marketplace** (payments, tax, reporting, DeFi, compliance, etc.) |

---

## Who it is for

- **Teams and individuals** who want AI to *do* financial and operational work, not only summarize it — with clearer control and traceability.  
- **Crypto-native users** who already live on-chain and want **agent subscriptions and payments** without losing the thread in generic tooling.

---

## Vision

**Money that moves with agents should be as legible as money that moves with humans** — same workspace, same receipts, same place to hire, fire, and fund the work.

---

## Closing line (investor / partner)

Beam is building the **default workspace for agentic finance on 0G**: marketplace, conversation, and treasury in one product, with chain and TEE-backed trust where it matters.
